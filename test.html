What I Do

Responsive Design

Your website will look stunning on every device — mobile, tablet, and desktop. I use fluid grids and flexible images to ensure a flawless experience for all users, no matter how they browse.

Design to Code

I convert your Figma or Sketch designs into pixel‑perfect, semantic HTML & CSS. Every spacing, color, and interaction is faithfully recreated, so your final product matches your vision exactly.

Performance

Speed matters. I optimize images, minify code, leverage caching, and reduce render‑blocking resources. Your site loads fast, keeping visitors engaged and improving SEO rankings.

Accessibility

The web should be for everyone. I build with WCAG guidelines in mind, using semantic markup, proper ARIA labels, and keyboard navigation support, so your site is usable by all.
